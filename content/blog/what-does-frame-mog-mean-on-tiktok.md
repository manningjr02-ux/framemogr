If you've been on TikTok lately, you've probably seen comments like:

## Quick Definition: Frame Mog Meaning on TikTok

On TikTok, **frame mogging** means visually overpowering someone in a photo or video because of your body structure, posture, and proportions.

To be **frame mogged** means:

You look smaller, narrower, or less dominant next to someone else in the same frame.

It's not about being ugly.
It's not just about height.
It's about **relative structure**.
